Privacy Statement

Natascha Hauser-Middelberg, established in Amsterdam and registered with the Chamber of Commerce under number 82064008, attaches importance to the protection of personal data. This privacy statement explains how Natascha Hauser-Middelberg handles information about an identified or identifiable natural person, as referred to in the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Application

This privacy statement applies to the following categories of natural persons whose personal data Natascha Hauser-Middelberg processes:

  1. (Potential) patients
  2. Visitors to Natascha Hauser-Middelberg's practice
  3. Visitors to www.inmijnhumm.nl
  4. Applicants
  5. All other persons who contact Natascha Hauser-Middelberg or whose personal data Natascha Hauser-Middelberg processes, with the exception of its employees.

Processing of personal data

Natascha Hauser-Middelberg processes personal data that:

  1. A data subject has provided information personally (during a discussion or meeting), by telephone, or digitally (via e-mail or web forms on the website), such as contact details or other personal data.
  2. With the consent of the person concerned, these can be requested from other care providers or referrers.
  3. During a visit by a data subject to the Natascha Hauser-Middelberg website, such as the IP address, surfing behavior on the website (such as data about the first visit, previous visit and current visit, the pages viewed and the way in which the website is navigated) and on which parts the data subject clicks.
  4. That have been obtained for e-health applications.

Purposes of processing

Natascha Hauser-Middelberg processes personal data for the following purposes:

  1. Carrying out a medical treatment agreement and the declaration for work performed.
  2. Maintaining contact, through invitations to meetings and information that the data subject has requested.
  3. Improving the practice website www.inmijnhumm.nl
  4. Keeping track of user statistics. User statistics of the website provide information about the numbers of visitors, the duration of the visit, which parts of the website are viewed and click behavior. These are generic reports that cannot be traced back to individual visitors.

Legal basis

Natascha Hauser-Middelberg processes personal data on the basis of one of the following legal grounds:

  1. Consent of the data subject. This consent can always be withdrawn, without affecting the lawfulness of the processing based on the consent before the withdrawal.
  2. Execution of - or with a view to - concluding a medical treatment agreement, including invoicing to third parties, such as the health insurance company, etc.
  3. A legal obligation, such as the obligation to keep a medical file or register the BSN.
  4. A legitimate interest, such as the use of contact details to invite you to a meeting.
